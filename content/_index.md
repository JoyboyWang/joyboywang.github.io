# About Me
Hi, I am **Yinqiao Wang**. 


I'm currently a 3rd-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at **the Chinese University of Hong Kong**, supervised by Prof. [Chi-Wing FU, Philip](https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~cwfu/) and Prof. [Pheng Ann Heng](https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~pheng/). I obtained my Master's degree from Shandong University, supervised by Prof. [Yunhai Wang](https://yunhaiwang.net/).

My research interests are human-object interaction, VR/AR/XR and egocentric world model.
