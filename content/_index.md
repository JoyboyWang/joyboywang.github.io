# About Me
Hi, I am **Yinqiao Wang (王银桥)**. 


I'm currently a 3rd-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at **the Chinese University of Hong Kong**, supervised by Prof. [Chi-Wing FU, Philip](https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~cwfu/) and Prof. [Pheng-Ann Heng](https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~pheng/). Prior to that, I obtained both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from **Shandong University**.

My research interests include human-object interaction, VR/AR/XR, and egocentric world model, particularly, learning to interact with the physical/virtual world using two hands.
